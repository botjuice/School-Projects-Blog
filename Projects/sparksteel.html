SPARK STEEL

Building the ultimate robot!

We are Spark Steel, a group of students who love to create and program robots. We work with Raspberry Pi using Python, a powerful and versatile platform for robotics. We design and build our own robots using 3D printing, actuators, and sensors. We use our skills and creativity to solve real-world problems and challenges. You can see our projects and achievements, learn about our process and methods, and discover our future goals and plans on this website.
